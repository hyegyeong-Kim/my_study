Chapter 1: General Provisions
Article 1 (Purpose)
The purpose of this Agreement is to stipulate the rights and obligations of members and the company, as well as the terms and conditions for using all services within the SK mySUNI service platform provided by SK Innovation (hereinafter referred to as the “Company”).
Article 2 (Definitions)
  • ① The terms used in this Agreement are defined as follows:
    • 1. “Service” refers to all services provided by the Company for SK mySUNI members. The overview of these services is described in Article 3 of this Agreement.
    • 2. “SK mySUNI Member” (hereinafter referred to as “Member”) refers to an individual who, after accessing the website (https://mysuni.sk.com), agrees to this Agreement and is granted a user number (Member ID) and sets a password through the procedures recognized by the Company in order to receive SK mySUNI services. This Member ID is part of the integrated ID system, allowing the Member to access various services.
    • 3. “Member” can utilize the integrated authentication service, allowing them to log in to individual services using a single ID and password where the integrated ID system applies.
    • 4. “Integrated ID” (hereinafter referred to as “Member ID”) refers to a combination of characters, numbers, and symbols set by the customer or assigned by the Company for the identification of the Member and service usage. The Member can use this ID to log in to other services with the same identifier.
    • 5. “Password” refers to a combination of characters, numbers, and symbols set by the Member to confirm that they are the rightful owner of the Member ID and to protect the Member’s rights.
    • 6. “Integrated Login” refers to the act of using the Member ID and password to apply for services, use services, and check detailed information about services.
    • 7. “SK mySUNI Homepage” (hereinafter referred to as “Homepage”) refers to the web pages providing SK mySUNI services and related information, where all services within the SK mySUNI platform can be accessed via integrated login with the Member ID.
  • ② Except for the terms defined in Paragraph 1 of this Article, other terms used in this Agreement shall follow relevant laws and service usage guidelines.
Article 3 (Overview of SK mySUNI Services)
  • ① The services provided by the Company to Members under this Agreement are as follows, and customers wishing to use the services must complete the membership registration process stipulated in this Agreement. However, depending on the nature of individual services, some services may have restricted usage conditions or require certain qualifications, which the Company will notify the Members of.
  • ② For the smooth provision of services, the Company operates the homepage (SUNI https://mysuni.sk.com, Icheon Forum https://www.icheonforum.com), and Members can access various services provided on the homepage. However, Members wishing to use the SK mySUNI service homepage must complete the user information registration process, such as specifying their Member ID and Password as determined by the Company.
Article 4 (Amendment of Terms)
  • ① This Agreement may be amended from time to time. If the Company intends to amend the Agreement, it will notify Members of the fact that the Agreement has been amended and provide the details of the amendment through one or more of the following methods at least seven days prior to the effective date:
    • 1. Notification via email
    • 2. Notification via written document
    • 3. Posting on the SK mySUNI service homepage (http://mysuni.sk.com)
  • ② If the Company notifies Members of the amendment via email or written document, it will send the notice to the most recently provided email address or mailing address of the Member.
  • ③ Amended terms, in principle, become effective from the effective date onward..
  • ④ Members who have objections to the amendment may terminate their membership. However, if a Member does not terminate their membership within 30 days of the notification in accordance with Paragraphs 1 and 2, the Member is deemed to have agreed to the amended terms.
  • ⑤ The notification methods and effectiveness of notifications specified in this Article also apply to individual or general notifications as stipulated in other provisions of this Agreement..
Chapter 2: Membership Registration and Termination
Article 5 (Membership Registration)
  • ① Customers who wish to become Members must apply for membership by entering their personal information into the membership application form after agreeing to this Agreement and a separate ‘Consent to Collection, Provision, and Use of Personal Information’ on the homepage. Customers must use their real name for membership registration, and only one membership application per person is allowed.
  • ② The Company may limit or defer approval of membership applications in the following cases:
    • 1. If the name provided is not a real name
    • 2. If the application is made with the intent to violate public order or morals
    • 3. If false information is entered on the application form or if false documents are attached
    • 4. If there are technical or operational issues affecting the Company’s ability to provide services
    • 5. In other cases deemed inappropriate for membership by the Company
  • ③ Members may not transfer, lend, or use their membership status as collateral for third parties.
  • ④ By clicking the “Agree” button after completing the registration process, the Member is deemed to have agreed to this Agreement.
Article 6 (Membership Termination and Loss/Suspension of Qualification)
  • ① Members may request to terminate their membership at any time in writing, by email, by phone, or by other means as determined by the Company. The Company will promptly carry out the necessary procedures to process the termination request.
  • ② The Company may suspend or revoke the membership qualification of any Member for the following reasons, with notice to the Member, for a period of one year.

    In the case of Item 2, membership will be automatically revoked without notice:

    • 1. If false information was provided at the time of registration
    • 2. If the Member is deceased
    • 3. If the Member no longer meets the conditions required for membership
    • 4. If the Member interferes with the use of the SK mySUNI service homepage by other Members or misappropriates information, thereby disrupting service operations
    • 5. If the Member uses the SK mySUNI service homepage to engage in acts prohibited by laws or this Agreement, or acts against public order and morals
    • 6. If the Member interferes with the electronic transaction order by disrupting the use of the SK mySUNI service homepage by other Members or misappropriating information
    • 7. If the Member violates any other obligations stipulated in this Agreement
  • ③ Members whose qualifications are suspended or revoked for the reasons specified in Paragraph 2 may submit an explanation showing that the cause of suspension or revocation was not due to their fault or negligence. In such cases, the Company will review the Member’s explanation and, if deemed valid, allow the Member to resume normal SK mySUNI service usage.
  • ④ The timing of membership termination or qualification loss as specified in Paragraphs 1 and 2 is as follows:
    • 8. The date of the membership termination request or the notification of loss of qualification
    • 9. In the case of loss of qualification due to death, the date of the Member’s death
  • ⑤ The suspension of membership qualifications under Paragraph 2 will be effective for one year from the date of notification of the suspension. The Company may restrict re-registration of Members whose qualifications have been revoked under Paragraph 2.
Chapter 3: Homepage
Article 7 (Homepage Services)
  • ① The Company operates the SK mySUNI service homepage to provide efficient services to Members, and Members can receive various services (hereinafter referred to as “Homepage Services”) through the SK mySUNI service homepage.
  • ② The Company shall not engage in acts prohibited by laws or this Agreement, or acts against public order and morals, in connection with the operation of the Homepage Services, and the Company shall do its utmost to provide products and services continuously and stably in accordance with the terms of this Agreement.
  • ③ The operation of Homepage Services shall be available 24/7 year-round, barring any operational or technical difficulties. However, this does not apply during periods of scheduled maintenance, which will be announced in advance.
  • ④ The Company may set specific time limits for the availability of certain Homepage Services. In such cases, the Company will notify Members through service usage guidelines.
Article 8 (Content of Homepage Services)

The Company, through its Homepage Services, provides the following services:

  • 1. Provision of various information related to SK mySUNI services to Members
  • 2. Services for education and certification
  • 3. Services for the operation of the Icheon Forum and Ulsan Forum
  • 4. Other services as determined by the Company
Article 9 (Interruption of Homepage Services)
  • ① The Company may interrupt the provision of Homepage Services in the following cases:
    • 1. For maintenance, repair, replacement, or malfunction of computer or telecommunications equipment, or in case of communication outages
    • 2. If telecommunications services are interrupted by the telecom service provider as stipulated in the Telecommunications Business Act
    • 3. In the event of natural disasters, national emergencies, or other force majeure events
  • ② If the Company intends to interrupt the provision of Homepage Services due to the reasons specified in Paragraph 1, it will notify Members by email or post a notice on the SK mySUNI service homepage.
Article 10 (Member ID and Password)
  • ① Members who wish to use Homepage Services must register their Member ID and Password on the SK mySUNI service homepage according to the Company’s guidelines.
  • ② The management of the Member ID and Password is the sole responsibility of the Member, and Members shall not disclose or share their ID and Password with third parties.
  • ③ If a Member’s ID or Password is stolen or used by a third party, the Member must immediately notify the Company and follow the Company’s instructions.
Article 11 (Copyright and Restrictions on Use)
  • ① The copyrights and other intellectual property rights to materials created by the Company on the homepage are owned by the Company.
  • ② Content created by Members during the service, such as assignments, posts, and comments, may be freely quoted and used by the Company.
Article 12 (Deletion of Posts)

The Company may delete materials posted or provided by Members on the homepage without prior notice if it determines that the materials fall under any of the following categories:

  • 1. Content that defames or slanders other Members or third parties, or damages their reputation
  • 2. Dissemination of false information
  • 3. Content that violates public order and morals
  • 4. Content that infringes upon the intellectual property rights or other rights of others
  • 5. Content that does not conform to the purpose of SK mySUNI services
  • 6. Content that is deemed to be in violation of relevant laws
Article 13 (Dispute Resolution Regarding Homepage Service Use)
  • ① The Company will make every effort to promptly address complaints and opinions submitted by Members in connection with the use of SK mySUNI services. However, if immediate resolution is difficult, the Company will promptly notify the Member of the reasons and the expected timeline for resolution.
  • ② Disputes between the Company and Members arising from the use of SK mySUNI services may be submitted to the Electronic Documents and Electronic Transactions Dispute Mediation Committee established under the Framework Act on Electronic Documents and Electronic Transactions.

The Company provides Members with learning content, including video content, and networking services. The Company may add or modify the content of the services as necessary.

  • ⑤ In the event of system failures or loss of facilities, the Company will repair or restore them without delay to ensure continuous service provision. However, in cases of force majeure such as natural disasters or national emergencies, the service may be temporarily interrupted or discontinued, and the Company will notify Members without delay.

  • ① Members must provide accurate information when registering, and ensure that their information is maintained and updated to remain accurate.
  • ② Members shall not disclose or misuse any confidential information, including security information, posted on the homepage. The Company’s security information includes all technical and management information used in its operation, management, research and development, and service provision, and is considered confidential.
  • ③ Members shall not engage in the following activities:
    • 1. Submitting false information during membership registration or service application
    • 2. Modifying, copying, deleting, distributing, or commercially using any information posted on the SK mySUNI service homepage without the Company’s prior consent
    • 3. Posting or transmitting unauthorized information (such as programs or advertisements) via the SK mySUNI service homepage
    • 4. Infringing on the intellectual property rights of the Company or third parties
    • 5. Defaming the reputation of the Company or third parties, or interfering with their operations
    • 6. Posting obscene or violent information (messages, images, sounds, etc.) or content that violates public order and morals on the SK mySUNI service homepage
    • 7. Using another person’s Member ID or Password
    • 8. Engaging in activities that violate relevant laws
  • ④ Members are responsible for managing their Member ID and Password (which must be at least 10 characters long and include letters, numbers, and symbols, and must not use personal information or simple characters). If a Member’s Password is exposed to third parties, it must be changed immediately. Members are responsible for any consequences resulting from their failure to manage or misuse their ID and Password. If a Member’s ID is used fraudulently, they must notify the Company immediately. Members must comply with this Agreement and relevant laws. The Company may immediately terminate the contract or restrict the Member’s use of services if these provisions are violated, and may claim damages arising from such violations.
